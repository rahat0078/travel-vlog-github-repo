Sylhet Tour

Sylhet is the tea capital, a prime attraction for all tourists visiting Bangladesh. Lies between the Khasia and the Jaintia hills on the north, and the Tripura hills on the south. Sylhet breaks the monotony of the flatness of this land with a multitude of terraced tea gardens, rolling countryside, and exotic flora and fauna. This is home to the largest Shrines in the region, a land of vibrant cultures from tribal & local people. Its natural resources like a rainforest, swamp forest, wetlands, waterfalls, Tamabil, Jaflong, Lalakhal, and the Bichanakandi-connected to Meghalaya by the river are most interesting to visit.

Most beautiful places that you must visit in Sylhet

  • Ratargul Swamp Forest
  • Jaflong
  • Sreemangal
  • Bholaganj sada pathor
